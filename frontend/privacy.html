Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Bicol University Polangui Academic Platform

This platform is an academic tool developed for Bicol University Polangui students. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Who We Are

BUSimInt (Bicol University Simulation Interview) is an AI-powered interview practice platform developed as an academic project at Bicol University Polangui. It is not a commercial service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use BUSimInt, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture, collected via Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0).
  • Interview Transcripts: Text from your simulated interview sessions, including both AI interviewer questions and your typed or spoken responses.
  • Audio Data: If you use Voice Mode, your audio is sent to our backend for transcription (via Groq Whisper). Audio is not stored — only the resulting text transcript is retained.
  • Session Metadata: Interview type, job title, experience level, timestamps, and performance scores.
  • Usage Data: Basic logs for debugging and system reliability. No behavioral tracking or advertising data is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the interview simulation service.
  • To generate AI-powered performance analysis and feedback.
  • To store your session history so you can review past interviews.
  • To authenticate you securely using your Google account.
  • To improve the platform for academic purposes (aggregated, anonymized usage only).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

BUSimInt uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — for database storage of session data. Data is stored on secured servers.
  • Groq (Whisper) — for audio-to-text transcription in Voice Mode. Audio is processed and discarded; only transcript text is returned.
  • Google Gemini / Groq LLM — for generating interview questions and performance analysis. Prompts include interview transcripts but not your personal account information.

5. Data Retention

Interview sessions and transcripts are retained in your account until you delete them or your account is removed. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the platform administrator.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards including HTTPS encryption, secure token-based authentication, and access-controlled database storage. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

7. Children's Privacy

BUSimInt is intended for use by Bicol University students (typically 18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years of age.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time by discontinuing use of the platform.

To exercise these rights, contact the platform administrator through your Bicol University channels.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the BUSimInt development team through Bicol University Polangui's academic department.
